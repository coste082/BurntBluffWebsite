Meet the Daytons

After moving around the country for work as Geologists since college we are happy to be back to our home state of Wisconsin! Kari has been visiting Door County since she was a baby, spending summers in Egg Harbor with her grandparents. Ryan was hooked after his first visit and it was always a dream of ours to open a shop here and blow glass and make rock art. Our two shop assistants, Ida (5) and Piper (7) are all in on our family venture and have been painting, gardening and helping us make repairs to get the store ready to open.

We purchased the cabin in 2020 after years of driving by it thinking it was pretty darn cute sitting out there in the field. Its small size and some logistical issues due to its age (it was built in the Civil War era) led us to build a hotshop and workshop out back. We have had issues with acquiring equipment due to COVID related delays, but can’t wait to start blowing glass and making rock décor once everything arrives.

We try our hardest to source from other Wisconsin small businesses whenever we can. Our first year while we wait for our equipment to arrive, we are filling the store with things that make us happy, a lot of them WI and Door County themed. Once our hotshop and workshop are completed we will start making our own glass and rock art. We can’t wait! OUR GRAND OPENING WILL BE IN SUMMER 2021.

The next time you visit beautiful Fish Creek stop by our fun store. We would love to have you!
